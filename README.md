# 🎮 RPS Arena (Rock • Paper • Scissors)

> Dare to play. Outsmart the AI. Rule the arena.

A modern, interactive **Rock Paper Scissors game** built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, featuring smooth animations, AI difficulty levels, sound effects, and a responsive design for both mobile and desktop.

---

## 🕹 Play Online

👉 Click here to play:  https://piyushgupta389.github.io/Rock-Paper-Scissors/

### 🎯 Game Modes
- 🤖 **AI Mode** – Play against smart AI
- 👥 **Player vs Player (PVP)** – Play with a friend on the same device

---

### 🧠 AI Difficulty Levels
- 🟢 **Easy** – Random moves
- 🟡 **Medium** – Partially predicts your moves
- 🔴 **Hard** – Analyzes your pattern and counters intelligently

---

### ⚡ Gameplay Experience
- Instant player move response
- AI "thinking" delay
- Smooth animations & transitions
- Result display with emojis & effects

---

### 🎨 UI/UX
- Modern **glassmorphism + neon style**
- Dynamic color feedback:
  - 🟢 Win → glow animation
  - 🔴 Lose → shake effect
  - 🟡 Draw → neutral glow
- Fully **responsive** (mobile + desktop)

---

### 🔊 Sound Effects
- Click sound on move
- AI reveal sound
- Win / Lose / Draw sound feedback  
*(Uses Web Audio API — no external files needed)*

---

### ⏱ Timer Mode
- Optional 30-second timer
- Visual countdown ring
- Color changes:
  - Yellow (warning)
  - Red (critical)

---

### 📊 Score System
- Live score tracking
- Separate scores for player & opponent
- Reset functionality

---

## 🕹 How to Play

1. Select game mode (AI or PVP)
2. Choose difficulty (if AI mode)
3. Click **Start Timer** (optional)
4. Pick your move:
   - 🪨 Rock
   - 📄 Paper
   - ✂️ Scissors
5. Watch the result and beat your opponent!

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3 (Animations + Responsive Design)**
- **Vanilla JavaScript**
- **Web Audio API**
